# Captain Starpants

**Captain Starpants** is a vertical scrolling space shooter featuring wacky, overpowered power-ups, ridiculous bosses, and a wide variety of silly enemies.

The game is easy to pick up and play, but the real challenge comes from **achievements, high scores, and unlocks**.

---

# Game Concept

- **Genre:** Vertical Scrolling Space Shooter
- **Style:** Arcade / Score Attack
- **Tone:** Silly, chaotic, overpowered fun
- **Progression:** Unlock levels and characters by collecting stars

### Core Gameplay

- Ships match the **personality and style of each protagonist**.
- Instead of dying immediately, the player **loses power-ups when hit**.
- Players must collect **stars** to unlock new levels.

Stars are earned by:
- Defeating enemies
- Achieving high scores
- Collecting items

⚠️ It is possible to **finish a level but fail to unlock the next one** if the player doesn't collect enough stars.

---

# Characters

Bosses are **former friends of Captain Starpants**.

Defeating a boss **unlocks that character as a playable ship**.

Each character has:
- Different **starting stats**
- Unique **cosmetics**
- A different **alternate attack**

### Playable Characters

- **Captain Starpants** — Intrepid space explorer
- **KRUTON** — Robot destroyer
- **Shell Bell** — Space mermaid
- **Princess Ebby** — Presumptive heir to the universe
- **Angel Lis** — Extra-dimensional being
- **Jazzy Jen** — Latest winner of the pangalactic dance contest
- **???** — Secret character

# Power-Ups

## Basic Power-Ups

Collect **up to 5 levels** of each.  
These last until the player is hit.

- Speed
- Fire Power
- Pickup Distance

---

## Advanced Power-Ups

- **Weapons:** up to 3 levels
- **Companions:** 1 at a time
- **Powers:** 1 active + 1 standby

---

# Weapons

## Primary Weapons

- **Solid** — Long range solid beam (widens with upgrades)
- **Wide** — Short range wide arc (more shots per level)
- **Sword** — Short range semicircle slash (range increases)
- **Chain** — Standard shot ricochets (more bounces)

## Secondary Weapons

- **Whip** — Energy whip (longer reach)
- **Pulse** — Circular attack (faster recharge)
- **Ball** — Plasma ball (larger size)
- **Blade** — Yo-yo blades (faster movement)
- **Robot** — Short range slice (faster attack)

# Powers

Players may have **one active power and one in reserve**.

## Core Powers

- **Overload** — Basic powers to level 10, advanced powers to level 3
- **Annihilate** — Destroy everything on screen
- **Absorb** — Damage is absorbed
- **Magnet** — All items auto-collected
- **Reflect** — Enemy shots bounce off player

# Global Powers

Players can unlock **persistent upgrades** between levels by collecting enough points/money.

### Unlockable Global Upgrades

- Power-ups appear more frequently (by type)
- Enemies are worth more points (% bonus)
- Basic power-ups start at higher level (up to half)
- **Shield** — Each level grants protection from one hit

# Design Goals

- Easy to pick up and play
- Chaotic and overpowered gameplay
- High replayability
- Score chasing and achievements
- Large variety of powers, characters, and enemies
